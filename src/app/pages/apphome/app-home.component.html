devshift: spin all day

DEVSHIFT started as a 2-man ride session (mainly Cromwel Penaranda (Cavite) and Wilfredo Bibar (Paranaque)) way back in 2017. This was NOT actually the first group name we thought about before. Since we both live inside PH's southbound vicinities, we tagged ourselves as 'SouthBoys' before, although that aint good to hear (just saying). After a year, 2018 to be exact, we invited a couple of friends and co-workers which did enjoy cycling as well (Joenas Biocarles and Joma Silvestre). First, we thought of having to create our first jersey then brainstorm on team name that every member would agree on. Chatted for hours then finally, it went out, DEVSHIFT. All members are mostly web developers and frankly, we all have messed up working schedule anyway and to which that name intricately fits.
